Why Donate Blood?

Blood donation saves millions of lives every year. One single donation can help up to three patients in critical need.

Who Can Donate?

Most healthy adults between 18-65 years weighing at least 50kg can safely donate blood every 3 months.

The Process

The donation process takes about 30-45 minutes in total, with the actual blood draw only taking 8-10 minutes.
